Slayer Espresso Machines

The Slayer espresso machine is a statement of style and a step in the right direction towards espresso perfection. Its pre-infusion technology has taken the coffee world by storm, allowing the barista to slowly ramp up the water pressure, to make a more well-rounded shot of espresso.

Bob Moog invented the very first synthesizer, a towering behemoth of a machine that made all sorts of bleeps and bloops.
