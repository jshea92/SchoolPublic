The Classic

Developed by Mr. Les Paul himself, and used by legends like Billy Gibbons and Slash, this beast of a guitar has remained the king for decades.

Generally used in rock and hard music, the Les Paul uses the advantage of darker wood and humbuckers for its heavy tone. Competitors include: Fender Stratocaster & Telecaster, Gibson SG.
