About Us

Methodical Coffee opened on February 11th, 2015 with pennies in the bank. We opened witha line out the door that stayed out the door until we closed. We believe that any successful business is built upon three pillars: exceptional product, customer service, and experience. We tirelessly work to perfect those three pillars. Methodical Coffee is owned by David Baker, Will Shurtz, and Marco Suarez. David handles our operations, Will is our roaster and coffee leader, and Marco designs our brand and experiences.
